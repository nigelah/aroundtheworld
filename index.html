How to play
Around the World
• Visit 6 cities, answer questions at each stop, and build your prize fund.
• Answer 3 questions correctly at each city to complete it. Get them right and earn coins. Get them wrong and the host deducts coins.
• Flying between cities costs coins based on distance. Choose your route wisely — run out of coins and you're eliminated.
• Complete a city and the host gives you a clue to your next destination. Follow the clue to the right city.
• Use 50:50 to eliminate two wrong answers, making it easier to pick the right one. Costs 10 coins.
• Use Phone a Friend as a lifeline to reveal the correct answer. You get 3 on Easy, 2 on Medium, and 1 on Hard.
